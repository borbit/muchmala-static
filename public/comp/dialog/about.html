Muchmala α

This is an unusual jigsaw puzzle you can play with other people online. The difference form the usual jigsaw puzzle is that all pieces are initially collected to the wrong place. To finish puzzle you should swap pieces with the same shape.
Game is being built with powers of HTML5 and Node.js.

Created by Serge Borbit (@borbit)
