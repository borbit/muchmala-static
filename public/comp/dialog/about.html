Muchmala α

This is a unique jigsaw puzzle you can play with other people online. The difference with this unique puzzle is that all the pieces are initially in the wrong place. To finish the puzzle, you should swap pieces with the same shape. This game is being built with the powers of HTML5 and Node.js.
